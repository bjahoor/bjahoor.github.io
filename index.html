Draw a Path — Control the Arm

How it works Draw any path on the canvas (click & drag). A simplified planar 3-link arm uses a fast FABRIK inverse kinematics solver to make the end-effector follow your path.

Tips • Draw slowly for smoother paths
• Click Play to start following the path
• Click Reset Arm to return to home pose
• Click Clear Path to sketch a new trajectory

Under the hood 3-link demo approximates the Panda. You can replace this with your Panda kinematics later (same UI).
